Ferry — Bodø → Moskenes (Sun Jul 6)

Operator: Torghatten Nord  ·  Crossing: ~3 h 15 min  ·  Book ASAP — summer sailings with cars sell out weeks in advance.

Goal: arrive Stamsund (Steineveien 17) before 18:00 for dinner & check-in. Moskenes → Stamsund is ~1 h 30 min (~102 km). Working backwards, target the early-afternoon sailing:

Weather & Daylight — Early July
Midnight Sun in Lofoten — the sun does not set from late May to ~July 17. Expect full daylight at 02:00. Bring a sleep mask. Great for late-night photography and hiking; terrible for jet lag.

    Why 2-Day Return Fails
    1
    Lofoten → somewhere midway
    ⏱️ 15–18 hrs driving
    You'd need to reach roughly Trondheim in one day. That means non-stop driving from morning until midnight. For a convoy with EVs needing 2-3 charging stops — impossible.
    2
    Trondheim → Oslo
    ⏱️ 6–7 hrs
    The second day is fine — but you'd arrive at night 1 completely burnt out, especially after a full Lofoten trip.
    Bottom line: Skip this option. 3-day return is the same number of extra nights, but everyone arrives home fresh.

    EV Charging — Key Points
    Convoy note: Don't drive all 4 cars glued together. Plan regroup points every 2–3 hours so EV cars can charge while petrol cars wait nearby.
    Sweden route has much better EV infrastructure — Kiruna and Umeå both have multiple fast-chargers. Norway mountain roads have sparse charging.
